Recipe #1. Pancakes

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 4-5
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1.5 cup
Sugar 1 tablespoon
Eggs 3
Milk 500 ml
Boiled water 250 ml
Salt 1 pinch
Sunflower oil 2 tablespoons

Why should you cook it?

  • Many serves
  • Very tasty
  • Your boyfriend will marry you

Step 1: Put eggs, sugar and salt in a bowl and mix thoroughly.

Step 2: Add warm milk and mix.

Step 3: Add flour in several stages and mix well after each addition.

Step 4: Add hot water and mix the dough fast.

Step 5: At the end add an oil.

Step 6: Fry the pancakes in a well-heated frying pan until golden brown on both sides.

Bon appetit!

Recipe #3. Fried eggs with tomatos and onion.

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 3
Tomato 2-3
Onion 1
Salt 1 pinch
Sunflower oil 1-2 tablespoons

Why should you cook it?

  • Very fast
  • You always have all ingredients at home
  • Why not?

Step 1: Cut the onion into small pieces and the tomatoes into slightly larger pieces.

Step 2: Using a fork, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt.

Step 3: Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Fry the onion for a few minutes until soft. Add tomatoes and cook for another 3-4 minutes. Season with salt.

Step 4: Pour the beaten eggs over everything, stir and cook for a few more minutes.

Bon appetit!
